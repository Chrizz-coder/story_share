# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Last updated June 07, 2026**

---

## Refunds

**All sales are final and no refund will be issued.**

Story Share provides digital services including proposal creation, premium features, and subscription plans. Due to the nature of digital goods and services, all purchases are considered final once processed.

We strongly encourage you to review your plan and the features included before completing your purchase. If you have any questions about what is included in a plan, please contact us before purchasing.

---

## What This Means

- **No refunds** will be issued after a purchase is completed
- **No partial refunds** for unused portions of a subscription period
- **No credits** in lieu of refunds
- **Subscription cancellations** take effect at the end of the current billing period — you will not be charged again, but no refund is issued for the remaining period

---

## Exceptions

While all sales are generally final, we may consider exceptions in the following rare circumstances at our sole discretion:

- Duplicate charges caused by a technical error on our platform
- Charges made after a confirmed cancellation

To request consideration of an exception, please contact us within 7 days of the charge with your order details.

---

## How to Cancel a Subscription

To cancel your subscription and prevent future charges:

1. Log in to your Story Share account
2. Navigate to **Settings → Billing**
3. Click **Cancel Subscription**
4. Confirm the cancellation

Your access to premium features will continue until the end of the current billing period.
